About Me

Tell us a little about your story...

We started out with a passion to create the unimaginable in Kenya. Since then we have seen even larger breakthroughs than we could imagine. Through strategic partnerships and collaborations we have created a wide and efficient network in the field of architectural design.

Why architecture and design?

Architecture and design has influenced our way of life, recreational activities and comfort.It has further evolved through millenia to become what it is today. SImply put its a millenia of undying passion for me.

What do you do when your not designing?

When I'm not designing buildings you will definetely find me photographing something. My passion for creativity is undying and I hope I ignite the same passion in you.
